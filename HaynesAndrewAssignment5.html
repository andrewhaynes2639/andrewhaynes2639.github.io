Overview

Sudoku is a very fun game that I enjoy playing from time to time. Whenever I have down-time, flying on an airplane, or just bored at work I can complete some sudoku puzzles. I really enjoy math so this is quite fun for me and also keeps me continually utilizing my critical thinking skills.

Playing the Game

The basic layout consists of a square table layout with a size of 9 cells high by 9 cells wide. From there it further separates the layout by splitting into 9 smaller squares that are 3 cells high by 3 cells wide. Each row and column can contain numbers 1-9, however they cannot be repeated. The smaller 3 x 3 blocks can only contain numbers 1-9 in each of them. While figuring out the puzzle, you want to get the numbers 1-9 in each row, each column, and each 3 x 3 block with no repetitions in either 3 of those areas. This is quite tough sometimes, as they make different difficulties of sudoku puzzles.

Repeat

Sudoku consists of a lot of repeating. I typically scan the puzzle from top-to-bottom and from left-to-right. This means that I begin in the top left, and begin moving right to see if I can figure out any of the cells. Once I finish the first row, I move on to the second row, and so-on so-forth until I reach the bottom. Once I reach the bottom, I do it all againâ€¦and sometimes again, and again!

End result

As the end result you would like to fill in every block with a number. When you think you are done, you need to check to make sure you did not have any repeated numbers. The most important result, of course, is that you did not fall asleep while completing your sudoku puzzle. Have fun!
